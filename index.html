The 1 key is assigned to and is set to mode.

Every 6 achievments completed will earn you a new hotkey slot. Hotkeys can be used to trigger buttons just by pressing keys on your keyboard. You can set hotkeys to many different actions, such as adding points, buying buyables, resetting, etc. (You can only set it to buttons you have unlocked, of course). Hotkeys have three different modes: Single, repeat, or rapid-fire. Single will only activate when you start pressing the button, repeat will act like how holding a key normally works (activates once, waits a short delay, and then reactivates quickly), and rapid-fire will activate every tick that the key is held down. Certain hotkey functions will force you to use a specific mode, such as changing base requiring you to use single.
